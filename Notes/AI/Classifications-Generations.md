- **General AI:** A theoretical state where a machine can perform any intellectual task a human can.

---

### 2. The Agentic Era (2025 Trend)

We have moved past simple "Chatbots" into the era of **Agentic AI**. 
* **Narrow AI** responds to a prompt.
* **Agentic AI** takes a goal (e.g., "Research this topic and write a report") and independently plans steps, uses tools (browsers, code interpreters), and executes the workflow without human hand-holding.

---

### 3. Biological Frontier: Organoid Intelligence (OI)
Often referred to as **"Organ-intelligence"** or biological wetware:
* **Definition:** A field that combines **3D cultures of human brain cells (organoids)** with AI interfaces.
* **Goal:** To create "biocomputers" that are millions of times more energy-efficient than silicon-based chips. 
* **Key Advantage:** Biological systems excel at "few-shot learning" (learning from very little data) where silicon AI requires massive datasets.
